App Name: Clean Kangaroo

Slogan: Clean Laundry at Your Door

Tutorial Steps:

1. Choose your preferred local laundry store from dozens of options nearby. View ratings and pricing per item for each store.

2. Add your credit card and delivery address. We'll pick up your clothes within 30 minutes of your request.

3. Once your laundry is all nice and clean, we'll drop it off at your earliest convenience right at your door! Easy!
